Stores To Try!

CHIP CITY

Home to the best freshly baked cookies you'll ever find!

Their cookies are made fresh out of the oven and ready to serve. While they are a little pricy, do not fret because you are definitely getting a delicious cookie in returns. With their vast variety of flavors, you can believe that you won't want to eat a boring chocolate chip cookie ever again

AMPLE HILLS

The best way to cool off is with some ice cream from Ample Hills!

Ample Hills is a one of a kind ice cream shop. They have so many flavors to the point where you can't have a favorite flavor. From Cotton Candy to their "Ooey Gooey Butter Cake", you will taste ice cream like never before. With NYC being such a humid and hot place during the summer, what else could you possibly want besides Ample Hill's ice cream.

BAREBURGER

One of the best burgers you'll find in Astoria!

Despite being located in such an obscure place, they get a good amount of customers everyday and it makes sense. Judging from their name, you can assume that they are known for their delicious burgers. Their fresh burgers are levels above your typical fast food burgers, and is comparable to burgers from well known chains like Shake Shack. Not only do they serve burgers and fries, but chicken wings as welll; all fresh.

MOM'S KITCHEN

A quiet diner that is home to everyone and anytime!

Mom's Kitchen isn't your run of the mill diner. Whether you want your daily breakfast waffles or your strip steak, they got it all. Their Heavy Metal Fries are to die for, along with their Jumbo Chicken Wings. Everything is made to appeal to your taste buds, and they get it right everytime. They even have different types of salads if you're feeling a little healthy that day.

SABOR DE CUBA

An amazing place to get a taste of Cuba!

Sabor De Cuba has a menu like none other.Their lunch menu alone holds dishes that could definitely pass for something a Michelin restaurant would have. Their Vaca Fritas or their Picadillo Havana are both really good dishes, along with their other entrees. This is definitely one of those places that you just HAVE to try at least once.

Butcher Bar

If you're a meaty person, Butcher Bar has all the types you'd ever want!

Last but not least is a place called Butcher Bar. No, it's not an actual butcher but rather an amazing restaraunt that lives up to its name. They have meats of all kinds, cooked in various ways in order to suit the customer's desires. Their appetizers alone are quite the hefty dish for one so you already know that the main course will be worth your money and time. They have smoked ribs, different types of steak, burgers, and even sandwiches. With their endless supply of meats, it's all up to what your mind is craving for.
